Frequently Asked Questions

When does the tournament take place?+
The Avesham Season 2 tournament will be held across multiple weekends starting from next month. Specific dates will be communicated via email after booking.
Can I get a refund if I can't attend?+
Yes, refunds are available up to 7 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to another person with prior notification.
What facilities are available at the venue?+
The venue features food courts, restrooms, first aid stations, parking facilities, and designated seating areas. Season ticket holders get access to VIP lounges.
How will I receive my tickets?+
All tickets are delivered digitally via email within 24 hours of booking. You can print them or show the digital version on your phone at the venue.
Is there an age restriction for entry?+
Children under 5 years get free entry but must be accompanied by an adult. All other attendees require a valid ticket regardless of age.
